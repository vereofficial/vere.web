Daily Sidequests

Quick ideas when you need them. 2-15 minutes. Swipe right to accept, left to skip. Use your daily swipes wisely. Once they're gone, the deck is locked until tomorrow.

Example Daily Sidequests:

  • The Thrift Find: Go to the nearest thrift store and find the ugliest hat possible. Send a selfie in the Pod. (+25 XP)
  • The Side-Eye: Go to a cafe and write a 1-sentence "overheard" mystery. Make it as dramatic as possible. (+30 XP)
  • The Reviewer: Buy a snack you've never tried before. Give it a professional 1-10 rating in the chat. (+20 XP)

Weekly Challenge

One week. One challenge. Delivered every Sunday. Everyone gets the same challenge. Main character energy.

Example Weekly Challenges:

  • Memory Bank - "Choose a large set of information you're genuinely curious about and memorize it over the course of a week."
  • Name Game - "Introduce yourself to strangers around campus or your neighborhood until you meet someone whose first name starts with the same letter as either your first or last name."
  • Paper Trail - "Hunt down a flyer or poster advertising an upcoming event near campus, then actually show up."

Weekend Sidequests

Bigger adventures for when you have time. Unlock Friday at 5pm. Higher stakes, higher rewards50-80 XP.

Example Weekend Sidequests:

  • The 5-Star Tour: Find a 5-star rated "hole in the wall" food spot within 5 miles. You must go with at least one Pod member. (+65 XP)
  • The Ghost Town: Visit a local landmark or park after 8:00 PM and take a cinematic "liminal space" photo. (+55 XP)
  • The Skill Swap: Spend 30 minutes having a friend teach you something they are good at (skating, gaming, cooking). Proof required. (+75 XP)
