Sidekix Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026 · Effective Date: January 28, 2026

Sidekix (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and takes it seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Sidekix app, website, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate and improve Sidekix.

a. Information You Provide

  • Name or display name when you create an account
  • Profile details such as interests, preferences, or group participation
  • Content you choose to submit, including challenge responses or messages

b. Usage and Device Information

  • App activity such as feature usage, session duration, and interactions
  • Device and technical data including IP address, operating system, device type, and browser

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the Services
  • Personalize challenges, groups, and experiences
  • Improve product performance and usability
  • Communicate updates, notifications, or important service information
  • Maintain platform safety and enforce community standards

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Information Sharing

We may share information only when necessary:

  • With service providers who help operate the app
  • To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests
  • To protect the rights, safety, or integrity of Sidekix and its users

Partner experiences or sponsored challenges may involve limited data sharing, clearly disclosed at the time of participation.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable, industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. No system is completely secure, and use of the Services is at your own risk. Users are encouraged to protect their account credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the Services or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to applicable laws.

6. Children’s Privacy

Sidekix is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

7. Third-Party Links

The Services may include links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties and encourage users to review their policies independently.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or website. Continued use of the Services after updates constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy can be directed to: sidekix.team@gmail.com
